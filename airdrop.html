About

A cryptocurrency airdrop is a marketing strategy used by blockchain-based start-ups to distribute new tokens or coins to a wide range of individuals in the crypto community. The teams behind these projects often use airdrops to raise awareness of their project and encourage people to become users or investors. Airdrops are often associated with the launch of a new cryptocurrency or a DeFi protocol.
